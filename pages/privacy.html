Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines how we handle data on ErrorLog.

1. Data Collection

ErrorLog is a static repository. We do not collect personal identification information from our visitors. We do not require account registration to access the bug-fix database.

2. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes. We may use essential browser storage to remember your search preferences locally on your device.

3. Third-Party Services

Our site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect certain technical logs as part of its hosting services. Please refer to GitHub's Privacy Statement for more information.

4. External Links

Our articles may contain links to external documentation or GitHub repositories. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external sites.
